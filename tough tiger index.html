More Than a Haircut

At Tough Tiger, we believe a great barbershop is more than sharp scissors — it's a place where you feel at home. Our barbers bring old-school craft into every session, from classic fades to traditional hot towel shaves.

We keep things personal. Every client is greeted with good conversation, complimentary coffee, and a cut that's built around you — not just whatever's trending.
